Chicken Fried Rice

Description

Our chicken fried rice is a perfect blend of savory flavors, tender chicken, and fluffy rice, all stir-fried to perfection.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 25 mins

Ingredients
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup cooked chicken
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cups peas and carrots
  • 1/4 cup green onions
  • 2 tbsp soysauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • Salt and pepper
Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: Calories 350; total Fat 12g; saturated fat 2.5g; cholesterol 95mg; sodium 600mg; total carbohydrate 42g; dietary fiber 2g; total sugars 2g; protein 18g; vitamin c 8mg; calcium 30mg; iron 2mg; potassium 220mg
