Echo Harmony

A genre-blending musician known for ethereal vocals and atmospheric soundscapes that fuse indie folk with electronic elements. With haunting melodies and introspective lyrics, their music evokes a sense of nostalgia and wonder. Rising from the underground scene, Echo Harmony has gained a devoted following through emotionally charged performances and dreamy production. Their latest work continues to push sonic boundaries, creating a hypnotic experience that lingers long after the music fades.
